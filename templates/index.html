Our Purpose

By aggregating and parsing news from incoming sources, the public can remain informed and current on the lynchings being perpetrated in the Black Community. After enough time, the data collected could be used for statistical and criminal analysis. Once we have gathered enough information, legislature and reformation can be properly implemented in order to further protect the Black Community.
